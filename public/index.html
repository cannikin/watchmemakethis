Watch Me Make This

Give your clients a peek into the creative process

Watch Me Make This is a way for artists, craftspeople and designers to show pictures of their work as it's being made. Let a client watch as you create their dresser, jewelery or painting.

What is this?

Are you an artist? Craftsman / woman / person? Have you ever thought about letting a client watch over your shoulder as you're building something for them, minus the heavy breathing? Watch Me Make This is for you (and them).

How does it work?

Take photos while you're working and tweet, email or upload them to us. You get a website to send your clients to and they can follow along with your progress.

How much does it cost?

It's free!
